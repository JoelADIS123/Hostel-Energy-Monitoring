Legend for % values
Risk 1h % = estimated probability that the appliance will exceed its threshold in the next 1 hour.
0% to 40% = low risk, currently safer trend.
40% to 70% = medium risk, monitor closely.
70% to 100% = high risk, likely to exceed threshold soon.
P>Th 1h / P>Th 24h in Advanced ML = probability of crossing appliance threshold.
P>Budget 1h / P>Budget 24h = probability that room cost exceeds the budget you entered.
Budget Use % = predicted cost divided by budget.
Budget Left = budget minus predicted cost.
